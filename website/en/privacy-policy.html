Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection

We collect the following information:

  • Account information (e.g., name and email address)
  • Calendar data from connected calendars
  • Message content from connected WhatsApp accounts for event extraction
  • User preferences and app settings

2. Use of Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Jadwal app
  • Extract and manage events from your calendars and messages
  • Resolve scheduling conflicts and optimize your calendar
  • Send notifications about events and app updates

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can also opt out of certain data collection or use.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@jadwal.app
